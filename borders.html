Border width

The Width can be set as a specific size(in px, pt, cm, em, etc) or by using one of the three predefined values: thin, medium, thick

Specific side widths

The border-width can also has up to 4 values(top, right bottom left) given in a clockwise order

(5px 20px)5px top and bottom 20px on the sides

(20px 5px)20px top and bottom 5px on the sides

(25px 10px 4px 35px)25px top, 10px right, 4px bottom 35px right
